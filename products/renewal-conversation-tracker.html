Renewal Conversation Script & Tracker

Free Leasing Training template

Opening the conversation

"Hi [name], your lease is coming up in [timeframe] and I wanted to check in before we get into renewal details — how has your experience here been overall?"
Listen fully before pitching anything. This surfaces real concerns you can still fix before they've mentally checked out.

If they raise a concern

"Thanks for telling me — let me see what I can do about that before we talk numbers."
Address what's fixable. A resolved concern often matters more to the renewal decision than the price itself.

Presenting the renewal

"Your renewal rate is [$X], which reflects [brief, honest context — market conditions, property updates, etc.]. I'd love to have you stay — what questions do you have?"
Context, not just a number, makes an increase feel reasonable instead of arbitrary.

If they're undecided

"No pressure at all — would it help if I sent the renewal paperwork over now so it's ready whenever you decide?"
Make staying the path of least resistance. Removing friction often tips a resident who's on the fence.

If they decide not to renew

"Totally understand — would you mind sharing what led to that, just so I can pass it along?"
Exit feedback is some of the most honest information you'll get about what's working and not working.
